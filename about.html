From Research to Reality

Founded in 2019, AI IT Services began as a specialized consultancy focused on bridging the gap between theoretical machine learning and legacy enterprise architecture. We noticed a recurring pattern: companies were investing millions in AI prototypes that never reached production.

We pivoted to focus exclusively on 'Practical Adoption'—ensuring that every line of code serves a business metric. Today, we are a team of architects, engineers, and strategists who believe that AI is a tool for precision, not a solution in search of a problem.
